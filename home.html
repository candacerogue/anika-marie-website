About

From a semi-small town, I am Portsmouth, VA born and raised. I come from a very large family where I was the first to graduate from a 4-year college program. I stand on the shoulders of a strong support system; one that paved the way for me to attain all of my life goals, including applying to law school.

 

At 32, I am a serious candidate who brings more than professional accomplishments to the table and classroom; I also bring life experience and perspective. Hard work, integrity and service are engraved into the core of my being. I apply them in everything that I do as a wife, mother and servant.

Whether I am in the living room or boardroom, who I am are one in the same. If asked, friends, family and colleagues would describe me as compassionate, loyal, trustworthy, hard-working, reliable, calculated and tough but fair. My loving family, serving others, thinking through challenging situations and engaging in meaningful conversations make my life complete.

Thank you for your consideration. It is with humility and excitement that I embark on this new journey into a career in law.

 
— Anika Marie
